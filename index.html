About the Artist

Meet Areena

Helping you hold onto the moments that matter through pressed florals and quiet storytelling.

Hi, I'm Areena. I'm a botanical artist and slow maker who believes that the smallest moments often hold the most meaning. I press flowers for people who want to keep a feeling close: a wedding day, a memory of someone they love, or a season of life they never want to forget.

Through heirlooms, creative workshops, and quiet storytelling, I help transform those moments into something you can cherish. If you're someone who treasures meaning, beauty, and the in-between parts of life, you'll feel right at home here.
