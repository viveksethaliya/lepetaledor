Preserving Cherished Memories

We specialize in delicately pressing and preserving funeral bouquets, transforming them into timeless keepsakes that capture those precious memories. Each stem and petal is carefully arranged and framed, creating a beautiful and tangible reminder of the love and memories shared.

Our Process

Contact Us

Reach out to us via email to discuss your requirements and book our services. We're here to answer any questions you may have and provide assistance throughout the process.

Secure Your Booking

Once your booking is confirmed, we'll discuss payment options with you.

Drop Off Your Flowers

Bring or send us the funeral flowers or bouquet you'd like to preserve. We recommend doing this as soon as possible after the service to ensure optimal preservation.

Preservation Process

We will carefully press and preserve each flower, stem, and petal from your bouquet. The drying process takes 6-8 weeks.

Composition And Framing

Once preserved, the flowers will be thoughtfully arranged and framed to create a beautiful keepsake.

Your Keepsake Is Ready

We'll notify you as soon as it's ready for pick up or delivery.
